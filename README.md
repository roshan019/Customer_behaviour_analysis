# Customer Behavior Analytics Dashboard

A business intelligence and data analytics project that analyzes customer purchasing behavior, sales trends, subscription patterns, and demographic insights through an interactive Power BI dashboard.  

The dashboard enables data-driven decision-making by transforming raw transactional data into clear visual insights for revenue growth and customer strategy optimization.

---

## Project Overview

Understanding customer behavior is essential for improving sales performance and customer retention. This project provides a comprehensive analytics solution that:

- Tracks revenue and sales performance
- Identifies high-performing product categories
- Analyzes customer demographics and segments
- Monitors subscription adoption
- Evaluates customer satisfaction through ratings

The interactive dashboard allows stakeholders to explore insights dynamically using filters and visualizations.

---

## Objectives

- Analyze customer purchase patterns
- Measure key business KPIs
- Segment customers by age, gender, and subscription status
- Identify revenue-driving categories
- Provide actionable recommendations for business growth

---

## Dashboard Features

- KPI summary cards (Average Rating, Purchase Value, Total Customers)
- Revenue by product category
- Sales distribution by category
- Subscription status analysis
- Revenue and sales by age group
- Interactive filters (gender, category, shipping type)
- Dynamic and drill-down visualizations

---

## Tools & Technologies

- Power BI (Data visualization and dashboarding)
- Data Modeling & DAX measures
- Data Cleaning & Transformation

---

## Key Insights

- Clothing category generates the highest revenue and sales
- Young adults and adults contribute the majority of purchases
- Subscription adoption rate is relatively low
- Moderate average order value with growth potential

---

## Business Value

This dashboard helps organizations:

- Make data-driven decisions
- Improve marketing strategies
- Optimize product offerings
- Increase customer retention
- Enhance overall revenue performance
